Building Bridges Toward Career Opportunities for Millions of Americans

Overview

This repository presents my analytical breakdown of McKinsey & Company’s case on workforce development with Goodwill Industries International. 
The study focuses on streamlining career pathways, mentorship coaching, and scalable frameworks that foster meaningful employment opportunities for large populations.
